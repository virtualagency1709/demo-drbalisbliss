# Gall Stone Treatment in Ayurveda: Symptoms, Causes and Natural Solutions

Gallstones are hardened deposits forming in the gallbladder that can cause discomfort — and Ayurveda offers natural therapeutic approaches without surgical intervention.

## Symptoms of Gallstones

Common indicators include upper-right abdominal pain after eating, nausea, vomiting, back or shoulder discomfort and bloating. In rare cases, fever or yellowing of the skin may occur, and untreated gallstones risk gallbladder infection.

## Causes

Gallstones develop when bile becomes imbalanced — typically from excess cholesterol, poor digestion, obesity, hormonal changes or inactivity. Ayurvedic theory attributes this to a Pitta dosha imbalance, which governs digestion; maintaining Pitta equilibrium promotes healthy bile flow and prevention.

## Ayurvedic Treatment

The approach focuses on dissolution, better digestion and prevention through natural methods. Herbal remedies include Triphala for digestion, turmeric for inflammation, aloe vera for cleansing and punarnava for liver and gallbladder support. Diet and lifestyle emphasise frequent small meals, avoiding processed foods, more fibre, herbal teas and gentle yoga.

## Panchakarma, Hydration & Mind-Body Balance

Traditional Panchakarma cleansing with massage and steam strengthens digestive organs and eliminates toxins. Adequate water supports bile flow, and fresh juices like pomegranate provide antioxidants. Specific yoga poses enhance bile movement and digestion, while meditation, breathing techniques and consistent routines manage the stress that affects digestion.
