# Best GERD Treatment in South Delhi for Chronic Acid Reflux Relief

Acid reflux and heartburn affect many South Delhi residents — but natural, long-term management offers lasting relief beyond temporary medication.

## Understanding GERD and Its Causes

GERD occurs when stomach acid refluxes upward and burns the oesophagus. Common triggers include processed and spicy foods, irregular eating, weak digestion and emotional stress. Addressing the underlying causes — not just symptoms — leads to sustained improvement.

## Natural Ways to Manage GERD

Holistic approaches include dietary adjustments, increased hydration, lifestyle changes, gentle exercise and yoga, and herbal remedies like ginger and licorice — complementing professional treatment for better outcomes.

## The Role of Massage Therapy

GERD massage therapy supports digestive function, reduces bloating, increases stomach blood flow and promotes relaxation. Combined with dietary change, it forms part of a complete treatment protocol, and professional supervision helps prevent complications like oesophageal damage.

## Lifestyle Tips for Managing GERD

Eat smaller, frequent meals; avoid caffeine and carbonated drinks; elevate your head while sleeping; exercise regularly; and practise stress-reduction techniques like meditation.
