# Best Time to Start Post Natal Massage Therapy After Delivery

Knowing when to begin post natal massage helps new mothers recover safely and comfortably after delivery.

## When to Start After Delivery

For a normal delivery, gentle post natal massage can usually begin within a week or two — once you feel comfortable. After a C-section, it's best to wait until the incision has healed (generally a few weeks) and always with your doctor's guidance. Starting at the right time supports recovery without straining a healing body.

## Why Timing Matters

The postpartum body is restoring hormonal balance, healing tissue and recovering from the physical demands of birth. Beginning at the right time lets massage ease muscle soreness, improve circulation, reduce swelling and support relaxation without overwhelming the body.

## What the Therapy Involves

Therapists use warm oils and gentle, soothing techniques across the back, shoulders, legs and abdomen — stimulating feel-good hormones like oxytocin and serotonin, calming the nervous system and supporting better sleep and milk production.

## How Often & Aftercare

Most mothers benefit from two to three sessions a week during early recovery, tapering as strength returns. Stay hydrated, rest well, eat nourishing food and follow your therapist's guidance — and for C-section recovery, trained therapists use gentle techniques that avoid the incision area.
