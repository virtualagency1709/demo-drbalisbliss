# Healing After Childbirth with Post Natal Massage

Post natal massage offers physical, emotional and mental healing for new mothers recovering from the transformative experience of pregnancy and childbirth.

## The Physical Benefits

This therapeutic approach supports gentle recovery across multiple body systems — reducing muscle soreness and tension, enhancing blood flow, minimising swelling and fluid retention, promoting hormonal equilibrium, helping abdominal muscles recover, correcting posture, restoring vitality, easing tension headaches and improving skin quality.

## What to Expect

Sessions begin with a consultation about your health status, recovery phase and concerns, so therapists can customise the experience. The massage uses gentle, soothing techniques to release tension, boost circulation and support the body's natural healing — and patients often experience emotional relief alongside physical benefits. A calm environment with wholesome Ayurvedic meals supports extended recovery.

## Tips to Maintain Healing

Sustained wellness requires ongoing self-care — maintain hydration, eat nutritious food, practise gentle stretching or yoga, rest regularly, avoid strenuous activity, establish consistent sleep, breathe mindfully, follow your therapist's guidance, wear loose clothing, apply warm compresses and keep a gratitude journal for emotional health.
