# How Post Natal Massage Therapy in Delhi Supports Hormonal Balance Naturally

After childbirth, new mothers experience significant physical and hormonal changes — post natal massage therapy offers a natural, drug-free way to support recovery and restore balance.

## Understanding the Body After Childbirth

Hormonal fluctuations are constant through pregnancy and delivery. The postpartum body must restore hormonal equilibrium, reduce stress hormones, improve circulation, support lactation and heal tissue. Post natal massage provides gentle therapeutic support for this natural recovery.

## How Massage Restores Hormonal Balance

Hormonal imbalances after childbirth can cause mood swings, anxiety, fatigue and sleep problems. Massage stimulates feel-good hormones like oxytocin and serotonin, lowers stress hormones, improves relaxation and sleep, and supports milk production by calming the nervous system.

## Natural Oils & Key Benefits

Therapists use warm, soothing oils to nourish skin, promote circulation and reduce discomfort. Benefits include faster muscle healing, reduced swelling and water retention, improved joint flexibility, better sleep, enhanced emotional wellbeing and relief from neck, shoulder and back pain during early parenting.

## Care, Timing & Environment

Delhi facilities combine traditional healing wisdom with professional expertise and modern hygiene, customising sessions to delivery type and recovery needs. Massage generally starts 2–6 weeks after delivery (consult your provider first), with 2–3 sessions weekly during early recovery — best in a quiet, nurturing setting with proper nutrition. For C-section recovery, trained therapists use gentle techniques avoiding the incision area.
