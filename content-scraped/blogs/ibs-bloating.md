# Natural Ways to Relieve IBS Bloating Without Medication

Bloating from IBS can feel like a constant companion — but natural approaches that work with your body rather than against it can provide relief.

## Why IBS Bloating Happens

IBS is fundamentally a digestive disorder affecting the large intestine. Bloating occurs when gut processes become imbalanced — slowed motility or disrupted digestion — causing gas accumulation and uncomfortable abdominal pressure.

## Common Triggers

IBS-related bloating follows patterns that vary between individuals. Potential culprits include specific foods, rapid eating, stress, insufficient fibre or abrupt dietary shifts, inadequate hydration, hormonal fluctuations and prolonged sedentary behaviour.

## The Power of Natural Relief

Medication can help sometimes, but it doesn't always fix the cause. Natural solutions and detox therapies work with your body to calm the digestive system, enhance digestion and minimise inflammation — addressing root causes rather than temporary symptom management.
