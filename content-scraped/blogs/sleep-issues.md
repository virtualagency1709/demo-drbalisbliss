# Beat Sleep Issues Naturally with Detox, Nutrition and Mind-Body Therapies

Sleep problems impact energy, mood and health — but natural approaches combining detoxification, nutrition and therapeutic practices can restore healthy sleep without medication.

## Understanding Sleep Issues

Stress, poor nutrition, toxin build-up and irregular habits commonly trigger sleep disturbances, which compromise immunity, disrupt metabolism and raise the risk of chronic conditions. Identifying underlying causes is essential for resolution.

## Cleansing for Better Sleep

Bodily toxins can disrupt sleep. Far-infrared sauna, ozone therapy and colon hydrotherapy remove harmful substances while reducing inflammation and supporting digestion — all contributing to better rest. Detox programs help reset the body's internal clock naturally.

## The Role of Food

Nutrient-rich diets enhance the neurotransmitter function controlling sleep cycles. Foods with B vitamins, magnesium and tryptophan support relaxation, while processed foods, excess caffeine and late-night heavy meals worsen sleep quality.

## Mind-Body Techniques & a Sleep-Friendly Routine

Stress and mental fatigue are primary disruptors. Yoga, meditation and therapeutic massage reduce nervous-system tension and balance energy, while lymphatic drainage and bodywork improve circulation. Consistent routines — regular sleep times, limited screens before bed, an optimised bedroom, herbal teas and breathing exercises — prove most effective.
