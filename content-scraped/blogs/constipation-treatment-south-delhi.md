# Effective Constipation Treatment in South Delhi Designed for Long-Term Wellness

Constipation affects people of all ages and responds best to a holistic, sustainable approach rather than relying on over-the-counter medication alone.

## Understanding Constipation

Constipation develops when bowel movements become infrequent or difficult. Common causes include low fibre intake, dehydration, sedentary habits, stress and irregular eating. Ignoring it can affect daily life and overall health, so it's important to treat the underlying causes rather than just the symptoms.

## A Thorough Strategy for Relief

Lasting relief combines natural remedies, dietary guidance and lifestyle adjustments — increasing fibre-rich foods and hydration, regular exercise and yoga, Ayurvedic/homeopathic/naturopathic support, and detox programs such as colon hydrotherapy that reactivate natural peristalsis.

## Ease Bloating with Restorative Massage

Gentle lymphatic and abdominal massage techniques stimulate bowel movements, reduce bloating and improve digestive comfort — working best alongside dietary and lifestyle recommendations.

## Benefits of Specialised Treatment

Professional treatment offers personalised care, long-term relief focused on root causes, holistic wellness improvements and natural solutions that minimise reliance on harsh medications.

## Lifestyle Advice to Reduce Constipation

Drink around eight glasses of water daily, include probiotics, practise digestion-supporting yoga, eat high-fibre foods and eat mindfully to sustain long-term relief.
