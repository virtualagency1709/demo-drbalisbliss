# Step-by-Step Process of Ozone Therapy Treatment at Holistic Clinics

Ozone therapy has emerged as a natural treatment that strengthens immunity, improves oxygen circulation and supports detoxification within a personalised, holistic framework.

## Consultation & Body Assessment

Holistic clinics begin with a comprehensive consultation examining medical history, lifestyle, stress, digestion and immunity. Nutritional gaps are identified, and IV nutritional therapy may be recommended to prepare cells for improved oxygen absorption before treatment.

## Preparing the Body

The body undergoes gentle preparation through increased hydration, anti-inflammatory dietary adjustments and lifestyle modifications — enhancing receptivity and the effectiveness of treatment.

## Administration & Detox Phase

Medical-grade ozone is administered under expert supervision, with dose and technique selected for individual health and tolerance, improving oxygen transport and cellular energy. Treatment is often combined with detox programs supporting liver, kidney and intestinal function to neutralise harmful bacteria and break down toxin build-up.

## Circulation, Immunity & Monitoring

Better blood quality improves oxygen distribution; complementary yoga supports breathing and calm, while acupuncture may balance immune function. Physicians track vitality, digestion, sleep, pain and mental balance, adjusting dosage and guidance to individual response.

## Long-Term Integration

A dedicated homestay provides calm surroundings, structured routines, healthy nutrition and rest that amplify benefits. Post-treatment focus on mindful nutrition, hydration, exercise and stress reduction maintains the wellness gains over time.
