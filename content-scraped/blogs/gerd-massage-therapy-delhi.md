# Heal Naturally with GERD Massage Therapy in Delhi

Heartburn and acid reflux affect many people today — GERD massage therapy offers a natural alternative to medication by addressing underlying digestive issues.

## Recognising GERD and Its Causes

GERD develops when the muscle parting the food pipe and the stomach becomes weak or doesn't close properly, allowing stomach acid to rise into the chest. Common triggers include poor eating habits, stress, inadequate sleep and prolonged sitting. Rather than masking symptoms temporarily, this approach aims to restore digestive balance naturally.

## How It Works

The treatment targets the diaphragm and abdominal muscles through gentle, strategic adjustments to the chest and upper abdomen — reducing acid accumulation, improving circulation to the stomach and oesophagus, relieving diaphragm tension from stress or posture, and enhancing overall digestion.

## Benefits

Patients often experience less frequent and less intense heartburn, improved digestion with less gas, better breathing and posture, reduced stress-related reflux and better sleep. The therapy is non-invasive and works well alongside Ayurveda and naturopathy.
