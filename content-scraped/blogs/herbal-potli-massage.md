# What is Herbal Potli Massage and How Does it Work?

Traditional Ayurvedic herbal potli massage is a natural way to relax and recover — reducing tension and enhancing overall wellness by balancing body and mind.

## What is Herbal Potli Massage?

This ancient therapy places a blend of medicinal herbs and leaves inside small cotton pouches called potlis. The therapist applies these gently heated pouches using circular and tapping motions, letting warmth release the herbs' beneficial properties into the muscles and skin — addressing muscle tension, circulation, toxin removal and conditions like joint pain and stress-related fatigue.

## How Does It Work?

Potlis are warmed in oil or steam before application. The therapist uses gentle, rhythmic movements while heat opens the pores and enhances herb absorption. Common herbs include neem, eucalyptus, turmeric, lemongrass and camphor — each with specific benefits such as reducing inflammation or cleansing the skin — relaxing muscles and improving sleep.

## Benefits

The therapy relieves body pain, detoxifies tissue, reduces stress, improves skin health and boosts immunity. The combination of warmth and natural herbs promotes healing across physical, emotional and mental levels.

## When Should You Try It?

Ideal candidates have muscle or joint pain, poor circulation, fatigue, stress or dull skin. Many use it for recovery after demanding periods or integrate it into ongoing wellness routines — combined with Ayurveda, homeopathy, naturopathy and yoga for natural healing.
