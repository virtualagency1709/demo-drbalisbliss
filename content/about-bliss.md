### OUR JOURNEY

#### Where Health Begins: Our Story

Founded in 1996 by Dr. Poonam Bali, 'Bliss' began as a pioneering cosmetic skin clinic rooted in a holistic philosophy. Dr. Bali believed that true, lasting skincare results could only be achieved by addressing the Internal Health of the body. Combining her expertise in Cosmetic Medicine, gained from her studies in Cheshire, England, with her deep understanding of Natural Therapies like Aromatherapy, Detox, and Yoga, she created a unique blend of science and health.

Over the years, 'Bliss' has evolved into a comprehensive health destination, expanding its offerings to include Advanced Treatments such as Colon Hydrotherapy, Ozone Therapy, and IV Nutritional Therapies. With a luxurious center located at a prime location, 'Bliss' embodies Dr. Bali's vision of harmonizing Cutting-Edge Technology with Holistic Healing Principles. Today, it stands as a sanctuary for those seeking personalized care and transformative health, helping clients achieve balance, beauty, and vitality from within.
