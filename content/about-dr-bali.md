### Education & Professional Career
Dr. Poonam Bali, influenced by her Father's practice of Homeopathy and her Mother's focus on Naturopathy and Yoga, developed a natural inclination toward holistic and preventive healthcare from an early age. A consistent academic achiever, she chose to pursue Homeopathy, guided by her father's vision of its growing significance as an alternative medicine. While acknowledging the role of modern medicine in emergencies, for long-term, Dr. Bali advocates holistic solutions through Homeopathy and other alternative healing methods, emphasizing their effectiveness over medicine's short-term suppressive approach. Founded on principles of holistic well-being, she coaches her clients to support the Mind, Body, and Spirit through a personalized approach to health.

Being the top alumni of her prestigious institute, she was asked by her seniors to be a guiding and motivational force for the juniors. This led her to pursue teaching for some time. ### Dr. Bali's Unique Practice — A Health Coach and Cosmetologist
Dr. Poonam Bali is a trailblazer in holistic health and cosmetic dermatology, offering a unique blend of Homeopathy, advanced skin care techniques, and integrative health therapies to provide enduring solutions for skin, hair, and overall health. Unlike conventional approaches, she views her clients not as patients but as individuals experiencing temporary imbalances caused by Lifestyle, Diet or Genetics. With compassion and positivity, she employs Homeopathy as a foundation to restore balance, complemented by an array of holistic practices such as Detox Therapies, Ayurveda, Naturopathy, Yoga, Ozone Therapy, and Colon Hydrotherapy.

A gold medalist in Homeopathy and a visionary in cosmetic medicine, Dr. Bali founded 'Bliss' Total Health Center in 1996 to merge her passion for skin care and holistic health. She refined her expertise in cosmetic medicine in London, bringing cutting-edge technologies and products back to India, where she became the first practitioner to integrate alternative medicine with innovative dermatological techniques. Her practice quickly gained recognition for delivering permanent, root-cause solutions for a wide range of skin conditions, from acne and scars to pigmentation and anti-aging.

At 'Bliss', Dr. Bali combines advanced treatments like Dermapeels, Lasers, Non-Surgical Lifts, PRP Therapy, and Facial Rejuvenation with holistic therapies, including Yoga, Meditation, Aromatherapy, and Acupressure, to create customized health programs. Her introduction of Botox and fillers in collaboration with Allergan established her as a leader in aesthetic treatments, renowned for her artistic precision and gentle techniques.

Over the years, her clientele has expanded from Delhi to all parts of India and globally, catering to individuals across all age groups and lifestyles, from students and professionals to celebrities and dignitaries, including the late Rajmata Gayatri Devi. 'Bliss' has since evolved into a state-of-the-art multi-specialty holistic health center, offering a comprehensive range of treatments for skin, hair, and body, alongside stress management, weight loss, grooming, pre-bridal care, and lifestyle coaching.

Dr. Bali's commitment to lifelong learning and innovation has kept her at the forefront of holistic health care. As a trained Hatha yoga teacher and certified colon hydrotherapist, she continues to integrate ancient wisdom with modern practices, ensuring that 'Bliss' remains a sanctuary for transformative health and beauty solutions.

Today, 'Bliss' is celebrated for its "All Under One Roof" approach, offering unparalleled health and grooming services in a serene, state-of-the-art environment. Dr. Bali's dedication to empowering her clients to take charge of their health has made her a trusted expert and a true pioneer in the field.
