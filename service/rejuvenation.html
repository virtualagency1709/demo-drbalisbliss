Colon Hydrotherapy

The colon, or large intestine, is the body's main excretory ‘sewer’. When its function is impaired, old waste, faecal matter and gases accumulate. Periodical colon cleansing clears this build-up and rejuvenates the whole body.

Liver & Gall Bladder Cleanse

The liver and gallbladder hold the body's waste when digestion is poor. The liver provides energy through metabolism, but toxic layers deplete normal energy levels — so a liver and gallbladder detox rejuvenates the body's systems.

Detox Massages

Ayurvedic massages like Abhyangam, Udvartana, Shirodhara and Potli, together with Oriental and European bodywork — Deep Tissue, Swedish, Thai, Balinese and Acupressure — promote blood flow and detoxify the muscular tissues for a rejuvenating effect.

Our Ayurvedic Body Scrub deeply cleanses, exfoliates and rejuvenates the skin using herbal powders, grains, pulses and essential oils that gently remove dead cells, improve circulation and restore natural radiance — nourishing while it detoxifies. Regular sessions help with:

  • Stimulates blood circulation

  • Enhances natural glow

Steam Therapy

Steam therapy opens the pores, promotes sweating and flushes out toxins for fresh, glowing skin. It also improves circulation, eases muscular stiffness, supports respiratory health by clearing nasal and chest congestion, reduces stress, improves sleep and enhances natural immunity.
