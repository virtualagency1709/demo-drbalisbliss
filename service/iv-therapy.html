The Science of Absorption

What is IV Nutritional Therapy?

IV (intravenous) nutritional therapy is the delivery of vitamins, minerals, amino acids and antioxidants directly into your bloodstream through a gentle IV drip. By bypassing the digestive system, an IV infusion achieves near-complete absorption — far more than oral supplements, which often lose 50% or more to digestion.

At Bliss Holistic Health Clinic, every IV vitamin therapy session is preceded by a thorough assessment so that your drip is tailored to your unique deficiencies, energy needs and wellness goals. This is hydration and nourishment, perfected at the cellular level.

IV Therapy FAQs

Most guests feel only a small pinch when the IV is placed. Once the line is in, the infusion itself is painless — most people relax, read or nap throughout the 30–60 minute session.
Frequency depends on your goals and health status. Many guests start with a short series of weekly sessions, then move to a monthly maintenance drip. Dr. Bali will recommend a schedule tailored to you during your consultation.
When administered by trained medical professionals using clinical-grade nutrients, IV therapy is very safe. At Dr. Bali's Bliss, every infusion is physician-supervised and preceded by a health assessment to rule out contraindications.
Many guests feel more hydrated and energised within hours of their session, with benefits such as improved immunity and clarity building over the following days. Results are most lasting when IV therapy is part of a consistent wellness plan.
